About 27 Band

27 Band is a small game studio creating narrative-driven experiences with a strong sense of place. We like minimal UI, clear interactions, and worlds that reward careful attention.

We build games that feel like you’re walking through a memory — familiar, but slightly wrong.

What we value

  • Clarity Simple pages, fast loading, no fluff
  • Mood Atmosphere that supports gameplay
  • Intent Every mechanic has a reason to exist
